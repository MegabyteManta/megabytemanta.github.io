Project Overview

This is a collection of pieces I created while taking CGMA courses. I love making games, and part of that process is being able to put the worlds I create in my head onto the screen. Rigorously learning 3D art has allowed me to improve quickly and given me the opportunity to complete my games more fully.

I'm very inspired by Peru, which is my birth place, and its culture. A couple of the pieces I've made are inspired by ancient Peruvian ceramics, particularly those of the Nasca and Moche cultures. I think these ceramics are quite beautiful, and I hope to be able to share this ancient art form with a modern audience.

The CGMA courses I completed are Intro to Production Modeling, Vegetation and Plants, ZBrush for Concept and Iteration, Environment Art for Games in Unreal Engine, and Creating Stylized Game Assets.
