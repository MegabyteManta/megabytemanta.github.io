Project Overview

This is a collection of pieces I created on my own time. I've always wanted to be a great 2D artist so that I can be a better concept artist for my own worlds. Currently my favorite medium is toned tan paper.

My main inspirations are arthropods, plants, and ancient Peruvian pottery. I like art that is visceral, weird, and sometimes creepy. One of my favorite video games is Little Nightmares, which really captures the feel of the art I like to make. The last picture is a small snapshot of a scene from the game.
